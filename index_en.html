Introduction Lean More
Jianing Wang (1996-) is now an exam-free Ph.D student in East China Normal Unversity (ECNU). The main research scopes are Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing and Knowledge Graph, Few-shot Learning. He has achieved hornors on multiple competitions, such as 'Huawei Cup' Chinese Post-Graduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling, 'Huawei Cup' Smart Design Competition, Chinese College Students Computer Competitions, etc. He has published multiple papers in domestic and foreign conferences and journals includes CCF, core journals, etc. He has also joint the projects of Alibaba PAI, Oneflow, Zhijiang Lab. He has extensive internship experiences in multiple companies, such as Ant Group, Alibaba Clouds, etc.
